Vision & Mission

Vision

To create an exciting and impactful community-driven initiative that merges the thrill of motorsports with the joy of giving back.

Mission

To provide exhilarating drift ride-alongs and stunt demos that raise funds and awareness for Variety Children's Charity of BC, supporting children and families in need.

Marketing Strategies

Digital Marketing

  • Social Media Strategy (Zero Budget):
    • Facebook:
      • Create engaging event pages for each drift session
      • Join and participate in local car enthusiast groups
      • Share behind-the-scenes content of event preparations
      • Use Facebook Live during events to increase reach
      • Encourage attendees to tag and share their experiences
      • Post user-generated content and testimonials
    • Instagram:
      • Post daily Stories showing event preparation
      • Use relevant hashtags: #DriftBC #MissionRaceway #DriftCommunity
      • Share Reels of drift highlights and stunt performances
      • Collaborate with local car influencers for cross-promotion
      • Host Instagram takeovers with Hard Knox team members
    • TikTok:
      • Create trending content with drift footage
      • Share quick tips and motorsport facts
      • Post behind-the-scenes looks at stunt preparation
      • Use popular sounds and music to increase reach
    • YouTube:
      • Upload full event highlights
      • Create driver spotlight videos
      • Share charity impact stories
      • Post tutorial content about drift techniques
  • Community Building (No Cost):
    • Create a Facebook group for event participants and fans
    • Develop a volunteer social media team
    • Implement user-generated content campaigns
    • Host online challenges and competitions
    • Share success stories from charity beneficiaries
  • Content Calendar Strategy:
    • Monday: Weekly event updates
    • Tuesday: Technical Tuesday (car/drift content)
    • Wednesday: Community spotlight
    • Thursday: Throwback posts from past events
    • Friday: Weekend event promotions
    • Saturday: Live event coverage
    • Sunday: Impact stories and charity focus

Partnerships & Sponsorships

  • Collaborate with local businesses and car clubs
  • Develop sponsorship packages with clear value propositions
  • Offer promotional opportunities (logos on cars, banners, social media)
